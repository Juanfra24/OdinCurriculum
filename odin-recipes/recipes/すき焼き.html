すき焼き

A traditional Japanese hot pot with savory-sweet flavors

This dish is commonly enjoyed with friends and family. It combines thinly sliced beef, tofu, vegetables, and noodles cooked in a sweet-savory broth.

Ingredients:

  • 200g thinly sliced beef
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1 block tofu, cubed
  • 100g shirataki noodles
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 small napa cabbage, chopped
  • 2 shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 raw egg (optional for dipping)

Steps:

  1. In a hot pot, mix soy sauce, sake, sugar, and mirin. Bring to a boil and reduce to low heat.
  2. Add tofu, noodles, green onions, cabbage, and mushrooms to the pot. Let simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add beef slices and cook until just browned.
  4. Once everything is cooked through, serve immediately. For added authenticity, dip each bite in raw egg (optional).
