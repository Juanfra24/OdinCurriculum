焼き鳥

Japanese skewered chicken, perfect for grilling

焼き鳥 (Yakitori) is a popular street food and izakaya dish. It features skewered chicken grilled with a sweet-savory glaze. Perfect for a quick and delicious meal!

Ingredients:

  • 300g chicken thigh, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 2 Tbsp sake
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 2 green onions, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • Skewers

Steps:

  1. Soak skewers in water for 30 minutes if using wooden skewers.
  2. In a small pot, combine soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar. Simmer until thickened, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Thread chicken and green onion pieces onto skewers.
  4. Grill skewers over medium heat, brushing with sauce until chicken is cooked through and slightly caramelized.
  5. Serve with extra sauce on the side for dipping.
